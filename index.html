A complete AI-powered nutrition & calorie tracking platform — built full-stack with Node.js, Google Gemini multimodal API, and a premium green glassmorphism UI. Analyzes Indian food photos, generates personalized meal plans, and provides real-time dietary intelligence.

Feature 01

AI Food Image Analyzer

Upload any Indian dish photo — Gemini's multimodal vision identifies the food, estimates calories, calculates macros (protein/carbs/fats/fiber), and generates structured nutrition insights automatically.

Feature 02

Smart Nutrition Tracking

Manual food logging with real-time calorie tracking. Dynamic macro calculations with serving-size scaling, Indian food nutrition estimation, and daily progress visualization.

Feature 03

Personalized Health Engine

Computes BMI, BMR, and TDEE from age, height, weight and activity level. Sets personalized calorie and macro targets for fat loss, muscle gain, or maintenance goals.

Feature 04

AI Meal Planning Engine

Generates personalized Indian meal plans for fat loss, muscle gain, lean bulking, or maintenance. Supports vegetarian, vegan, South Indian, and North Indian dietary styles.

Feature 05

AI Nutrition Chatbot

Conversational nutrition assistant powered by Gemini API. Answers nutrition questions, recommends Indian foods, explains macros, and suggests healthier meal alternatives in real time.

Feature 06

Premium Dashboard UI

Green glassmorphism design system with animated dashboards, interactive charts, timeline-based tracking, and fully responsive layouts. A polished product, not just a demo.

I'm a Class XI student from Chennai who learns by building things that help me understand. Instead of just reading about the solar system, I built a 3D simulation. Instead of just using the web, I started making interactive apps.

I've been competing in Olympiads since Grade 4 — IMO, NSO, and NCO — not just for medals but because hard problems genuinely fascinate me. In 2023, I scored 59/60 in the IMO and ranked 2nd internationally. In 2025, I was recognised as a Merit Achiever in the Aryabhata Ganit Challenge, placing in the Top 100 students across the CBSE region nationally.

I spent the first two years at a primary school in Chennai, then a decade at a school in Anna Nagar, Chennai from Grade 1 through Grade 10. I recently joined a private school in Chetpet, Chennai for Class XI–XII, where I'm preparing seriously for JEE Main & Advanced.

On the technology side, I'm genuinely excited about what AI enables. I use ChatGPT, Claude, and Krea constantly — for debugging, brainstorming, and creative experimentation.

Long-term: crack JEE Advanced, study at IIT Madras or IIT Bombay, then pursue UPSC CSE to become an IAS officer. That's the plan. I'm working toward it.
