Feature 01

AI Food Image Analyzer

Upload any Indian dish photo — Gemini's multimodal vision identifies the food, estimates calories, calculates macros (protein/carbs/fats/fiber), and generates structured nutrition insights automatically.

Feature 02

Smart Nutrition Tracking

Manual food logging with real-time calorie tracking. Dynamic macro calculations with serving-size scaling, Indian food nutrition estimation, and daily progress visualization.

Feature 03

Personalized Health Engine

Computes BMI, BMR, and TDEE from age, height, weight and activity level. Sets personalized calorie and macro targets for fat loss, muscle gain, or maintenance goals.

Feature 04

AI Meal Planning Engine

Generates personalized Indian meal plans for fat loss, muscle gain, lean bulking, or maintenance. Supports vegetarian, vegan, South Indian, and North Indian dietary styles.

Feature 05

AI Nutrition Chatbot

Conversational nutrition assistant powered by Gemini API. Answers nutrition questions, recommends Indian foods, explains macros, and suggests healthier meal alternatives in real time.

Feature 06

Premium Dashboard UI

Green glassmorphism design system with animated dashboards, interactive charts, timeline-based tracking, and fully responsive layouts. A polished product, not just a demo.
